# How to Edit & Publish Your Website

Your Cairo By Nights website content — the **menu, prices, page text, photos, opening hours, and contact details** — is now managed in **Sanity Studio**. You can change anything yourself and publish it live, no developer needed.

---

## 1. Logging in

1. Go to **https://cairo-by-nights.sanity.studio**
2. Sign in with the Google account you were invited with (check your email for the invite from Sanity).
3. You'll see the **Cairo By Nights** content list on the left.

> Tip: bookmark that link. You can log in from a phone, tablet, or computer.

---

## 2. How the content is organised

The left sidebar is grouped to match your site:

- **Site Settings** — contact details, phone, email, address, opening hours, social links, footer text, and the header/footer menu links. *(One place — edits here show on every page.)*
- **Menu**
  - **Categories** — the menu sections (Entrees, Main Dishes, Teas, Wines, …) and their order.
  - **Items** — every dish and drink, with name, description, price(s), and photo.
  - **Banquet packages** — the $60 / $70 / $80 set menus (shown on both the Menu and Function Room pages).
- **Pages** — the editable text for each page: **Home, Menu page, About, Events, Location, Function Room, Contact, 404**.

---

## 3. Editing a menu item

1. Click **Menu → Items**.
2. Click the item you want to change (e.g. "Lamb Kofta (GF)"). Use the search box to find it fast.
3. Change any field:
   - **Name** — what the customer sees (keep dietary tags like "(GF)" in the name).
   - **Description** — the sentence under the name.
   - **Prices** — one row for a single price; add rows for things like wine (Glass / Bottle) or sizes (Small / Medium / Large). Type the amount **with the $** (e.g. `$32`).
   - **Image** — click to upload a new photo, or replace the existing one.
   - **Available** — turn off to hide an item without deleting it.
4. Click **Publish** (bottom right). Done — see step 6.

**To add a new item:** Menu → Items → **＋ Create** → fill in the fields → choose its **Category** → **Publish**.

---

## 3b. Making your menu live (or hiding it)

The full menu can be shown or hidden with one switch — handy while you're still finishing prices.

1. Click **Pages → Menu page**.
2. On the **Menu visibility** tab, find **"Show the full menu"**.
   - **ON** → visitors see the full menu.
   - **OFF** → visitors see the *"Our menu is being updated — please check back soon!"* placeholder.
3. Click **Publish**. The change is live within a few seconds.

> When you're ready to launch the menu, turn **Show the full menu ON** and **Publish**. To take it down again, turn it **OFF** and **Publish**. (If the site ever can't reach the content server, it safely shows the placeholder rather than a blank menu.)

---

## 4. Editing page text

1. Click **Pages**, then the page you want (e.g. **Home** or **Events**).
2. Each page is split into labelled sections (Hero, Welcome, FAQ, etc.) along the top tabs. Click a tab, edit the text, and it updates in place.
3. For paragraphs with **links** or **bold** words, select the text and use the toolbar (B for bold, the link icon for links).
4. Click **Publish**.

---

## 5. Changing contact details, hours, or social links

These live in **one** place so they update everywhere at once:

1. Click **Site Settings**.
2. Use the tabs (**General / Contact & hours / Navigation & footer**) to find the field — phone, email, address, opening hours, Facebook/Instagram, etc.
3. Edit and click **Publish**.

---

## 6. Publishing (making it live)

- After any edit, click the green **Publish** button (bottom-right of the document).
- Your change appears on the live website within a few seconds — just **refresh the page** on caironightsrestaurant.com.au.
- Nothing is live until you click **Publish**. While you're still editing, it's saved as a private **draft** that only you can see.

---

## 7. Undoing a change

- Every document has a **history**. Click the **⋯** menu (top-right) → **Review changes** to see past versions and restore an earlier one.
- To temporarily hide a menu item instead of deleting it, switch **Available** off and Publish.

---

## Quick answers

- **Will my edit break the design?** No. You only change words, prices, and pictures — the layout stays fixed.
- **Do I need to save?** Sanity auto-saves your draft as you type. You only need to **Publish** to go live.
- **I made a mistake after publishing.** Use **Review changes** (step 7) to restore the previous version, then Publish.
- **Something looks wrong on the site.** Refresh the page; live changes take a few seconds to appear.

Need help? Contact your developer.
